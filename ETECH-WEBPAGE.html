Poverty

remains a pressing global issue, affecting millions of lives and hindering the realization of human potential. The "No One Left Behind: Eradicate Poverty" project aims to address this challenge at the local level, specifically within the Saint Mary's University community.

Our Goal

is to foster awareness, empathy, and collective action to ensure that no one within our community is left behind due to economic hardships.

This advocacy project is aiming to create a campus-wide movement that not only sheds light on the complexities of poverty but also encourages students, faculty, and staff to actively engage in efforts to eradicate it. By fostering a sense of social responsibility, we aim to build a more compassionate and supportive community where the well-being of all members is prioritized.

This advocacy project will involve a series of events, workshops, and campaigns throughout the academic year. We will collaborate with campus organizations, faculties, and community leaders around the campus to provide insights into the root causes of poverty and strategies for sustainable change. Additionally, we will conduct awareness campaigns to challenge stereotypes and reduce stigmas associated with poverty, fostering a more inclusive and understanding environment.


How Everyone Can Support


Participate:

Attend workshops, seminars, and events organized by the project to deepen your understanding of poverty-related issues.


Advocate:

Use your voice to spread awareness about poverty and its impact on the community. Share information on social media, engage in discussions, and challenge misconceptions.


Volunteer:

Contribute your time and skills to local initiatives addressing poverty. Join community service projects or collaborate with existing organizations.


Donate:

Support fundraising initiatives to provide financial aid, resources, and opportunities for those in need within the university community.


Educate:

Take the initiative to educate others on the importance of eradicating poverty and the role each individual can play in creating positive change


By actively participating in this project, we can collectively work towards a Saint Mary's University community where every member has the opportunity to thrive, breaking the chains of poverty that may hold them back.
